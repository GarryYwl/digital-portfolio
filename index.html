About Me

I'm an Diploma in Information Technology Graduate at Nanyang Polytechnic with a focus on creating practical and user-friendly digital solutions. My journey into tech began with curiosity about how websites work and has since evolved into hands-on experience building full-stack applications, analysing data, and designing intuitive user interfaces.

I’ve worked with technologies like React.js, Node.js, and MongoDB to develop responsive web applications, and I also have experience using Figma for UI/UX design and Tableau for data visualization. Whether it’s developing a system from scratch or improving an existing feature, I aim to ensure that what I build serves real user needs.

I’ve contributed to both individual and team projects, including a full-stack resume automation system built in collaboration with SCCCI. These experiences taught me to balance technical problem-solving with clear communication and attention to detail. I'm always looking to learn, improve, and apply what I’ve learned to meaningful projects that make a difference.
